What is Dodgeball?

Dodgeball is the classic gym-class game where two teams face off, hurling rubber balls at each other while dodging incoming fire! In cartoon world, it's even more exaggerated—balls leave trails, characters spin in mid-air, and getting hit means a comedic spiral into the wall. It's chaos, laughs, and pure fun!

Cartoon Dodgeball Rules

  • Stay in bounds! Step out of the court and you're out.
  • Catch = Revival: Catch a thrown ball and a teammate comes back in.
  • Hit = Out: Get tagged by a ball and you're sitting on the sideline.
  • Head shots don't count (in friendly games).
  • Last team standing wins! Or the team with the most players when time runs out.

Why We Love It

Cartoon dodgeball captures that mix of competition and silliness. Nobody takes themselves too seriously—you duck, dive, and dodge with exaggerated moves, and everyone laughs when someone gets pegged. It's the perfect blend of gym class nostalgia and over-the-top animated action!
