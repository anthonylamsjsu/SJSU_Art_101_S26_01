Dear Carol,

As I am writing to you my dear-Carol, it is September 16 and I have finally made it from that shining port of New York to France! Sorry to say but our summer expeditures to Paris will have to be on hold for a while.

From what I have seen so far, Paris is not the same as what your mother had envisioned before the war and from what I can tell. It's not going to be the same for a long long time.
